Privacy Policy

INTRODUCTION
At AfriGate, we place the highest priority on the confidentiality and integrity of our users' data. This Privacy Policy outlines our rigorous standards for collecting, processing, and protecting your information in accordance with international data protection standards and the laws of the Democratic Republic of Congo.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

To provide a secure financial environment, we collect several types of information:
• Personal Identifiers: Full name, date of birth, nationality, and gender.
• Contact Data: Verified email address and mobile phone number.
• KYC Documentation: High-resolution digital copies of government-issued IDs, passports, and proof of residence.
• Technical Metadata: IP addresses, precise GPS geolocation data, device hardware specifications, and browser fingerprinting to detect and prevent fraudulent login attempts.

2. PURPOSE OF DATA PROCESSING

Your data is processed for the following mandatory reasons:
• Regulatory Compliance: To satisfy Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and Counter-Terrorist Financing (CTF) requirements.
• Security Operations: To monitor account activity for suspicious behavior.
• Marketing & Communication: To provide you with updates on new crypto assets, fee changes, and promotional offers. You may opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
• Customer Support: To resolve technical issues or transaction disputes efficiently.

3. THIRD-PARTY DISCLOSURE AND LEGAL OBLIGATIONS

AfriGate does not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. However, information may be disclosed under the following circumstances:
• Law Enforcement: We are legally obligated to share your data with the judicial authorities of the Democratic Republic of Congo upon receiving a valid legal request or subpoena.
• Banking Partners: To facilitate Fiat-to-Crypto transfers, limited data may be shared with regulated financial institutions.

4. DATA RETENTION AND ARCHIVING

In compliance with financial regulations, AfriGate maintains a strict data retention policy.
• Active Accounts: All data is kept for the duration of the business relationship.
• Closed Accounts: Your data and transaction history will be archived in a secure, encrypted "Cold Storage" environment for a period of ten (10) years following account closure to satisfy legal audit requirements before permanent deletion.

5. SECURITY INFRASTRUCTURE

We employ military-grade security measures:
• End-to-End Encryption: All data in transit is protected via SSL/TLS encryption.
• Internal Access: Access to user KYC documents is restricted to a limited number of "Security Cleared" personnel.
• Protection: Our servers are protected by advanced firewalls and 24/7 intrusion detection systems.

6. COOKIES AND ANALYTICS

We use tracking technologies, including Google Analytics and proprietary pixels, to analyze user behavior. This helps us optimize the platform's speed and security. By using AfriGate, you consent to the use of these tracking tools.

7. USER RIGHTS AND ACCESS

Under certain conditions, you have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you. Please note that for security reasons, certain data related to ongoing investigations or transaction logs cannot be deleted upon request until the legal retention period has expired.

8. AMENDMENTS

This policy is subject to change as regulations evolve. Continued use of the platform after any modification constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
